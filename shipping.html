Shipping & Returns


Shipping Policy

All orders are shipped within 3 working days. Our courier partner may take 5-10 days to deliver the orders to you. During festive offers there can be some delays.


Returns and Replacements

We strive to give you the very best shopping experience possible, however we do not accept returns. We are responsible to replace a product if it has reached you damaged or if the wrong product has been sent to you. Do inform us within 48 hours of delivery of the products.
